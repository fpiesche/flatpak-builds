Aleph One

Aleph One is the open source continuation of Bungie’s Marathon® 2 game engine. Available for macOS, Windows, and Linux, Aleph One supports Marathon, Marathon 2 and Marathon Infinity natively. Many third party scenarios and net maps are also available.
