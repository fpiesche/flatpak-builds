The Blue Bomber doesn't get too many new official games anymore, so it's been up to the fans to provide for themselves. Enter one of the best fan games released in a post-Mega Man 11 world with 2019's Rock N Roll here by Dennis Engelhard. The title is the gimmick in that you can play as Rock or Roll and take on the latest set of eight Robot Masters that have gone rogue against the world! While Mega has his usual charge shot, Roll doesn't, but her claim to fame here is that she gets herself a little double jump/hover.
