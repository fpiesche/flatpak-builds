Castle of Elite

Iji is an action-packed strategic platform shooter with large levels, powerful bosses, and lots of secrets. There are alternate gameplay events, dialogues and scenes depending on what you do, and a wealth of extras and bonuses. Features an original soundtrack by Chris Geehan and Dan Byrne McCullhough, Tom Mauritzon, and Captain Goodnight.
