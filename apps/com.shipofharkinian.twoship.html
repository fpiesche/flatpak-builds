2Ship2Harkinian does for Majora's Mask what Ship of Harkinian did for Ocarina of Time: a recompiled port that brings the original game to new platforms with a variety of fixes and improvements. We are careful to put any and all changes to the game behind toggles, so that by default you get the authentic experience.

  • Higher framerate - No longer constrained by 90s console hardware, your experience will now be as silky smooth as your modern GPU and monitor can handle!
  • Enhancements - Make your own fun! With a plethora of enhancements and cheats available, you're guaranteed to find a new way to play!
  • Randomizer - Shuffle what rewards you get from things like chests, skulltulas, and more with the built-in randomizer! Endless replayability! No patching required!

2Ship does not include any copyrighted assets. You are required to provide a supported copy of the game.
