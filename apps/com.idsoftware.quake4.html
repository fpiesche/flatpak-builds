Quake 4

Quake 4 is the true sequel to Quake II, continuing the Strogg vs. Humans storyline from the point where Quake II ended.

This is a Flatpak distribution of the official Linux port of the game, for ease of use as the game can be difficult to run on modern systems.

PLEASE NOTE

This Flatpak does not include the Quake 4 game data or a CD key. You will need to supply both. At least the files pak001.pk4 through pak012.pk4 from a retail installation of Quake 4 must be copied to $HOME/.var/app/com.idsoftware.quake4/q4base/.
