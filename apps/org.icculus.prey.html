Prey

Prey tells the story of Tommy, a Cherokee garage mechanic stuck on a reservation going nowhere. Along with his people, he is abducted to a menacing mothership.

This is a Flatpak distribution of Ryan C. Gordon's Linux port of the game, for ease of use as the game can be difficult to run on modern systems.

PLEASE NOTE

This Flatpak does not include the Prey game data or a CD key. You will need to supply both. At least the files pak000.pk4 through pak004.pk4 from a retail installation of Prey must be copied to $HOME/.var/app/org.icculus.prey/base/.
