dsda-doom is a successor of prboom+ with extra tooling for demo recording and playback, with a focus on speedrunning and quality of life.

dsda-doom includes many new features, such as:

  • Heretic, Hexen, MBF21, Doom-in-Hexen, UDMF, and MAPINFO support
  • In-game console and scripting
  • Full controller support
  • Palette-based opengl renderer
  • Debugging features for testing
  • Strict mode for speedrunning
  • Various quality of life improvements
  • Advanced tools for TASing
  • Rewind
